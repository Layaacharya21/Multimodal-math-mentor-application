Trigonometry Core Identities:
1. sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
2. 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
3. 1 + cot²θ = cosec²θ

Compound Angles:
- sin(A ± B) = sinA cosB ± cosA sinB
- cos(A ± B) = cosA cosB ∓ sinA sinB

Double Angles:
- sin(2θ) = 2sinθ cosθ
- cos(2θ) = cos²θ - sin²θ = 2cos²θ - 1 = 1 - 2sin²θ

JEE Pitfall: Pay close attention to the sign in cos(A+B); it uses a minus sign. Also, remember that tanθ is undefined at odd multiples of π/2.
